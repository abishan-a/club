About Us

The Science Club at Vavuniya Tamil Madhya Maha Vidhyalaya is a student-led initiative committed to exploring the frontiers of science and innovation. Our mission is to inspire curiosity, promote scientific thinking, and encourage hands-on learning through exciting projects, competitions, and outreach programs.
